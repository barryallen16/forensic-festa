From Sherlock Holmes to modern-day crime dramas, the pursuit of justice and the exhilaration of solving complex puzzles never fades. Join us as we delve into the world of investigation, where logic meets intuition, and every clue leads you closer to the truth. PLAY

Round Description

"Tech Minds, Quiz Time !"

Round 1 Rules

  • Time duration (30 minutes).
  • Set of questions will be provided and to be answered within given time

Round 2 Rules

  • Time duration (45 minutes)
  • Set of tasks related to cyber security (hash value, steganography, password cracking etc…) will be provided to the team who completes the task within given time will be selected to next round

Round 3 Rules

  • Time duration (2 hours)
  • Read the story provided carefully
  • Only 5 minutes will be given for each team to investigate inside the crime scene
  • You will be given autopsy report and and Suspect Description
  • Don’t touch or misplace the evidences
  • Any 2 members from the team can raise only 3 questions to the Suspects and Eye Witness.
  • Your final report points should be related to the story and killer must be identified with reasons mentioned.
