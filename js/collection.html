Forensic Festa

From Sherlock Holmes to modern-day crime dramas, the pursuit of justice and the
exhilaration of solving complex puzzles never fades. Join us as we delve into the world
of investigation, where logic meets intuition, and every clue leads you closer to the truth.
Prepare to test your detective skills, challenge your intellect, and experience the ultimate
dopamine rush as you unravel mysteries one piece at a time.

ROUNDS DESCRIPTION

  • Time duration (30 minutes).
  • Set of questions will be provided and to be answered within the given time.
  • Time duration (45 minutes).
  • Set of tasks related to cybersecurity (hash value, steganography, password cracking, etc.) will be provided. The team who completes the task within the given time will be selected for the next round.
  • Time duration (2 hours).
  • Read the story provided carefully.
  • Team members have only 5 minutes to investigate inside the crime scene.
  • You will be given an autopsy report and suspect description.
  • Don’t touch or misplace the evidence.
  • Any 2 members from the team can raise only 3 questions to the suspects and eyewitness.
  • Your final report points should be related to the story, and the killer must be identified with reasons mentioned.
